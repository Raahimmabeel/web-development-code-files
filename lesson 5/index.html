Facts about dogs

Dogs sweat through their paws, and their nose is unique like a human fingerprint, with a sense of smell at least 40 times more sensitive than ours. They have an intelligence comparable to a two-year-old child, can be right or left-pawed, and some breeds can even be trained to detect diseases like cancer. Dalmatians are born white and develop spots later.

They are called,a mans best friend.
